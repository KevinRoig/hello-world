About Us

Created in 1926, the Wild Circus is specialized in outdoor performances. We travel to mountains, forests, lakes and volcano galleries to provide a circus as you never seen it before! We traveled around the world about 12 times since our creation but we still enjoy going in unknown countries to provide a first class entertainment, and we hope we will meet you soon!
